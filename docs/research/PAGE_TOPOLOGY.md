# Page Topology: Jalecos Conforto

## Overview
- **Header:** Sticky on scroll, contains Logo, Global Search, Cart, Navigation.
- **Main:** Vertical flow of sections.
- **Footer:** Multi-column (Info, Institutional, Payments, Seals).

## Sections (Top to Bottom)

1. **Top Header / Benefits Text:** Promotion banner (Frete Fixo R$ 10,00).
2. **Main Header:** Logo + Search Bar + Customer Links + Cart.
3. **Navigation Menu:** (Hidden in Hamburger on Mobile).
4. **Hero Banner Carousel:** Full-width Swiper banners (Promotion "Tecido Two Way", etc.).
5. **Icon Benefits Bar:** Horizontal list of "Frete Fixo e Grátis", "Compra Segura", "Troca e Devolução".
6. **Menu de Cores:** Circular category links for scrubs by color (Azul, Rosa, Bordô, Cinza, etc.).
7. **Promotion Banner:** Single large image ("Modelos Clássicos").
8. **Mais Vendidos:** Product carousel/grid with 2 products per row on mobile, multiple on desktop. Cards have "Destaque" label, Title, Price, Ratings.
9. **Novidades:** Same structure as Mais Vendidos.
10. **Nosso Blog:** Cards with Title, Date, Subtitle, "Ver todas as notícias" button.
11. **Depoimentos:** Customer feedback section.
12. **Instagram Grid:** (Likely dynamic).
13. **Seals / Footer Info:** Certifications, Payment methods.
14. **Footer Bottom:** Institutional links and copyright.

## Dependencies
- **Sticky Header:** Adjusts `top` or background on scroll.
- **Z-Index Layers:**
  - `Header`: 100+
  - `Overlay/Modals`: 200+
  - `WhatsApp Floating Button`: 150+
